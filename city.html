INFORMATION ABOUT A CITY

SWITZERLAND

Switzerland is a federal republic located in Western, Central, and Southern Europe. It is bordered by
Italy to the south, France to the west, Germany to the north, and Austria and Liechtenstein to the east
1. The country is divided into 26 cantons, with federal authorities based in Bern 1. The country’s
population of approximately 8.9 million people is concentrated on the Swiss Plateau, which hosts the
largest cities and economic centers, including Zürich, Geneva, and Basel 1.

Switzerland is known for its scenic beauty, chocolate, cheese, watches, banks, and ski resorts 1. The
country is also home to several international organizations, including the United Nations, the World
Health Organization, and the International Olympic Committee 1.

Switzerland is one of the world’s most developed countries, with the highest nominal wealth per adult
and the eighth-highest gross domestic product (GDP) per capita 1. The country has four official
languages: German, French, Italian, and Romansh 1. The Swiss Confederation is a directorial republic
with elements of a direct democracy 1.
